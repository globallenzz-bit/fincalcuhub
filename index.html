About the 2028 US Presidential Election

The 2028 United States Presidential Election will be held on Tuesday, November 7, 2028. American voters will choose between Democratic and Republican nominees, as well as potential independent and third-party candidates. The winning candidate must secure at least 270 of 538 electoral college votes to become the next President of the United States. Key swing states including Pennsylvania (19 EV), Georgia (16 EV), Michigan (15 EV), and North Carolina (16 EV) will likely determine the outcome. This site tracks 2028 election polls, candidate profiles, debate schedules, and election predictions in real time.
